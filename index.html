What Is This?

This site is a showcase of the devices I use daily. Every page features a scroll-driven animation built from hundreds of individual WebP frames — no video files, no lazy loading. Just raw images rendered to a canvas as you scroll.

The entire site is built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No React, no Next.js, no Tailwind. Just clean code, handwritten from scratch. Because sometimes you don't need a framework to build something cool.

Each device page tells a story — the specs, the reasons behind the choices, and the experiences that came with them. Whether it's the custom PC build that took months of planning or the second-hand phone that refuses to die.
